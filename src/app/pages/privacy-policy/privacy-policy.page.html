Your privacy is important to Keystone Adventures

We attach great importance to protecting personal data and maintaining privacy. This policy describes the types of personal information we may collect from you, the purposes for which we use the information, the circumstances in which we may share the information and the steps that we take to safeguard the information to rotect your privacy.

The personal data we collect and why

When entering your data into the online registration form we collect this data from you for the purpose of registering you for an experiential/outdoor education rogram. Of course, you are not required to supply any of the information that we may request. However, failure to do so may result in our being unable to register you for the program.

Use and disclosure of your personal data

By submitting this registration form you consent to the processing of the entered information by Keystone Adventures as described in this privacy statement. We will use the personal information to process your registration and make arrangements for the program. The information will NOT be shared with third parties.

Controlling your personal data

You are entitled to access any personal data about you held temporarily by Keystone Adventures at any time. You are also entitled to have us modify or delete any information that you believe is incorrect or out of date.

Under normal circumstances, all of your personal information will be deleted 7 Days after the conclusion of the trip. The only exception to this is if there are any outstanding complaints, medical issues, etc, which might require us to keep your information for a longer period of time.
